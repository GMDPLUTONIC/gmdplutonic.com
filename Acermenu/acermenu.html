AcerMenu Showcase

Feature Spotlight

  • Geode Support - AcerMenu has Geode support meaning you can download other really useful mods or mods that have features that AcerMenu doesnt have (e.g. Grandpa Demon)
  • Auto-Saving - Hacks will be saved even after you close Geometry Dash so you can carry on where you left off
  • AcerBot - You can use this to record and replay macros. Only meant for showcasing and not for any hacking attempts like Spaceuk
  • StartPos Switcher - Switch between start positions with your Q and E keys (on android and ios press the buttons), no more dozen copies per level for practicing
  • Internal Recorder - AcerMenu comes with a Built-In Internal Recorder so you can record your levels to showcase them on youtube and if it doesnt work dont worry because it has obs in the mods resources folder

Supports: Windows, Mac (you need to play through wine), Ios (jailbreak needed), and Android, Geometry Dash 2.206 (Geode )
